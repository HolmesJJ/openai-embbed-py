question: How do I look out for hypoglycaemia?
answer: These are the common signs and symptoms of hypoglycaemia:
- Hunger
- Changes in behaviour (e.g., moodiness, confusion)
- Unconsciousness
- Tiredness
- Trembling hands/nervousness
- Sweating
- Dizziness/headache
