question: How do I look out for hyperglycaemia?
answer: These are the common signs and symptoms of hyperglycaemia:
- Feeling tired and weak
- Frequent urination
- Feeling of nausea and/or vomiting
- Blurred vision
- Feeling thirsty all the time
